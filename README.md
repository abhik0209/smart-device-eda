# 📊 Smart Device Market Analysis (EDA)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project explores the smart wearable market through **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**, focusing on devices like **smartwatches and fitness bands**.

The goal is to uncover insights into:

* Pricing strategies
* Brand positioning
* Product features
* Customer perception

Using Python-based analysis, this project highlights patterns in **price, ratings, battery life, display technology, and brand popularity** to better understand the current wearable tech landscape.

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes the following attributes:

* Brand Name
* Device Type
* Original Price
* Selling Price
* Discount
* Rating
* Reviews
* Display Type
* Average Battery Life
* Strap Material
* Color

---

## 📌 Key Analysis Areas

### 💰 1. Price & Value Analysis

Understanding how brands compete in the market:

* **Discount Trends:**
  Identifying brands offering the highest and most consistent discounts

* **Price vs Rating:**
  Exploring whether higher-priced devices actually receive better ratings

* **Luxury Segment:**
  Analyzing brands with the highest average original prices and their product distribution

---

### ⚙️ 2. Performance & Features

Evaluating how product specifications impact user experience:

* **Battery Life Distribution:**
  Identifying common battery ranges and detecting extreme outliers

* **Strap Material Preferences:**
  Understanding how materials like *Leather vs Silicone* influence ratings

* **Display Impact:**
  Comparing AMOLED vs LCD in terms of:

  * Selling Price
  * Battery Life

---

### 📊 3. Brand & Market Insights

Analyzing brand dominance and consumer trends:

* **Brand Popularity:**
  Measuring which brands receive the most reviews and whether popularity aligns with ratings

* **Color Trends:**
  Identifying the most common device colors and their pricing impact

* **Device Type Breakdown:**
  Comparing the proportion of smartwatches vs fitness bands

---

### 📈 4. Data Distribution & Quality Check

* **Price Skewness:**
  Analyzing whether selling prices follow a normal distribution or are skewed

This helps identify:

* Market structure (budget vs premium dominance)
* Presence of high-end outliers

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* The dataset is dominated by **smartwatches**, reflecting current market trends
* Selling prices show a **positive skew**, indicating a higher number of budget-friendly devices
* **AMOLED displays** are commonly associated with premium pricing
* High review counts do not always correlate with higher ratings
* Some brands consistently use **aggressive discount strategies**
